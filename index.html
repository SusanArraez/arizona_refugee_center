What we do

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to assisting refugees who have been resettled in Arizona. We provide a range of services designed to help newcomers establish safe, stable, and self-sufficient lives. From initial reception and housing to language training, job placement, and cultural orientation, our goal is to create a welcoming environment and provide the tools necessary for success and integration. We believe in the strength and resilience of every individual and work to empower them for a brighter future for all.
